The Beginning

Emmerse yourself in learning about the night sky and what's beyond our world! There are countless stars in this vast universe we live in. In this universe, there many mysteries, and many more to be discovered. Have you ever wanted to know more about the world around us, but didn't know where to start? This is the place for you! Here I introduce, explain and provide knowledge on anything related to astronomy. I have learning that's explained simpler for beginners and children. Additionally, I have learning that dives deeper and introduces some marvelous deep space wonders.

Explore

Stargazing has been known to reduce stress, improve mood, and increase a feeling of awe. It doesn't take very much time at all either! Just taking at least 5 minutes out of your day to look at the moon or the stars up in the sky is enough. Additionally, it can be very intriguing and fun!
